About

I am a political scientist working on environmental politics, migration governance, infrastructure, and the politics of knowledge production. My research combines insights from International Relations, Science and Technology Studies, and qualitative methodology to examine how categories such as "climate mobility" are produced, circulated, and made actionable in international politics.

Research Interests

  • Environmental politics
  • Climate mobility
  • Migration governance
  • International Political Sociology
  • Infrastructure studies
  • Science and Technology Studies
  • Qualitative and multi-sited methodologies
